The ELEKTRON Ritual Method

The method is a structured ceremonial progression designed to restore stability and embodied presence. Each phase builds on the one before it.

RESET → GROUND → SUSTAIN

  • Phase I — Reset: establish discipline, restore rhythm, prove consistency.
  • Phase II — Ground: stabilize what Reset revealed through containment and integration.
  • Phase III — Sustain: lifetime continuity standards and ongoing refinement (coming next).

The Steps Taken to Achieve the Goal

ELEKTRON works because it is built as a progression — not a one-time experience. This is the exact path:

  • Step 1 — Commit: choose a container (Reset is the primary entry).
  • Step 2 — Repeat: daily ritual engagement builds internal structure.
  • Step 3 — Reflect: weekly reflection turns experience into integration.
  • Step 4 — Complete: completion is earned, not assumed.
  • Step 5 — Progress: Ground stabilizes; Sustain maintains for life.

The work is not rushed. It is returned to.
