What It Is

The Ritual Invitation Box is a curated introduction to ELEKTRON — a small ceremonial set designed to bring you into rhythm with the body. Each box includes sample-size ritual products and a small amber stone to anchor intention.

Not a purchase. An invitation.

Contents may vary slightly based on seasonal availability while keeping the ritual experience consistent.

How to Use as Ritual

  1. Arrive. Clear a small space. Three slow breaths.
  2. Anchor. Hold the amber stone. Feel its weight and time.
  3. Open. Choose one sample and begin slowly — scent first, then touch.
  4. Embodiment. Let the ritual be quiet. Let it be yours.
  5. Close. Thank the moment. Place amber where you’ll see it again.

Grounded. Empowered. Whole.
