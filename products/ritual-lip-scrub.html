FAQ

What is Ritual Lip Scrub made for?

It is designed to gently exfoliate dry lips, smooth texture, and prepare the lips for hydration.

How often should I use it?

Use as needed. Avoid over-exfoliating if your lips are sensitive.

Is it safe if a small amount is ingested?

The ingredients are simple, but this product is intended for external use only.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Ritual Lip Scrub is for external use only.
