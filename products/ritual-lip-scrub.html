FAQ

What is Ritual Lip Scrub made for?

It is designed to gently exfoliate dry lips, soften rough texture, and prepare the lips for hydration.

How do I use it?

Massage a small amount onto the lips using light circular motions, then rinse or wipe away.

How often should I use it?

Use as needed for gentle exfoliation. Avoid over-exfoliating sensitive lips.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Ritual Lip Scrub is for external use only.
