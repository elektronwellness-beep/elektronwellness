FAQ

What is Ritual Renewal Butter made for?

It is designed to support moisture, softness, and restoration while also serving as part of a grounding body ritual.

How do I use it?

Warm a small amount between your hands and press into the skin after bathing or as part of your daily ritual.

Will it feel greasy?

Arrowroot powder is included to help reduce a greasy finish and create a smoother, more balanced feel on the skin.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Ritual Renewal Butter is for external use only.
