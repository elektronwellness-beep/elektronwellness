Ritual Renewal Butter

A rich amber-infused body butter designed to restore, ground, and seal the skin through intentional formulation.

Restore Balance Seal

Ritual Renewal Butter is a concentrated blend of plant-based oils and butters crafted to restore the skin while supporting a grounded, balanced state.

Benefits

  • Deeply hydrates and seals moisture
  • Supports the skin barrier with rich botanical nourishment
  • Leaves skin soft and conditioned without a greasy finish
  • Offers a subtle grounding ritual through amber infusion and essential oils

Description

Ritual Renewal Butter was created as a body ritual rather than a basic moisturizer. Built around coconut oil, cocoa butter, amber-infused avocado oil, and arrowroot, it melts into the skin while maintaining a smooth, velvety finish.

The formula is designed to support restoration after bathing, evening ritual, or any moment that calls for grounding and intentional care. It brings together softness, warmth, and presence in one concentrated preparation.

Ingredients

Coconut oil, cocoa butter, amber-infused avocado oil, arrowroot powder, vitamin E, frankincense, copaiba, cassia, lavender, peppermint, cedarwood, black pepper, and cypress.

Ritual

Warm a small amount between your hands and press into the skin after bathing or as part of your daily ritual. Focus on areas needing restoration, softness, and grounding.

Care

For external use only. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.
