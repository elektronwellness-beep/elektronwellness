Ritual Invitation Box

The Ritual Invitation Box is the entry point into the ELEKTRON system. It is designed to introduce ritual through touch, texture, warmth, and intentional daily practice.

This set brings together core elements of the ELEKTRON experience — body care, grounding, and symbolic connection — creating a simple but meaningful starting point.

It is not just a collection of items. It is a beginning.

A small natural piece of amber to serve as a grounding and symbolic anchor within your ritual practice.
