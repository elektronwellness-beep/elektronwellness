Ritual Spray

A grounding aromatic mist created to clear space, awaken the senses, and support intentional presence.

Ritual Spray is designed as a simple but powerful tool within your daily ritual practice. It can be used to reset a space, shift energy, or bring yourself back into focus and awareness.

Ingredients

Water, cedarwood essential oil, peppermint essential oil, cassia (small amount).

Use

Spray into the air, onto linens, or into your personal space. Can be used before meditation, during ritual, or anytime you want to reset your environment.

FAQ

What is Ritual Spray used for?

It is used to clear space, refresh energy, and support grounding and focus during daily life or ritual practice.

Where can I use it?

You can use it in your home, workspace, on linens, or in any personal environment where you want a reset.

Can I spray it on my body?

This spray is designed primarily for space and environmental use. Avoid direct contact with eyes or sensitive skin.

Why is cassia used in a small amount?

Cassia is a strong oil, so only a small amount is used to support warmth and activation without overpowering the blend.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Ritual Spray is for external use only.
