FAQ

What is 7-Day Grounding?

It is a short guided grounding experience designed to help you reconnect, stabilize, and return to intentional practice.

Who is it for?

It is for anyone who wants a shorter structured ritual experience focused on grounding and reset.

How is it different from the 30-Day Ritual Reset?

7-Day Grounding is shorter and more focused on immediate grounding and reconnection, while the 30-Day Ritual Reset is a broader and deeper structured practice.

How should I use it?

Move through it day by day as intended, allowing each practice to build on the last in a simple and consistent rhythm.
