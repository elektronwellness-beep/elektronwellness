Describe the aroma, the pacing, and the emotional tone of the cup. Make it earthy, calm, and ceremonial.

“Steep. Inhale. Sip.”

Ritual Use

  1. Scoop 1–2 tsp into an infuser (adjust to taste).
  2. Steep 5–8 minutes, covered.
  3. Inhale the aroma before the first sip.
  4. Sip slowly; let warmth settle the body.

Tip: Pair with your amber stone—tea becomes an anchor to time.
