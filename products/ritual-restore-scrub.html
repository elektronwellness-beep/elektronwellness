Exfoliation Ritual

Ritual Restore Scrub

Ritual Restore Scrub is an amber-centered exfoliating body ritual designed to support renewal, cleansing, grounding, and restoration through intentional care.

This offering includes two scrub variations, each with its own feel and purpose. One is bright and renewing, the other is deep and grounding.

Sugar + Epsom Ritual Scrub

Renew • Polish • Brighten

This scrub is designed for a brighter, more uplifting body ritual. It combines exfoliation with a renewing botanical profile and a softer, more polished feel.

Ingredients

Sugar, epsom salt, amber-infused avocado oil, ground rosehip, amber powder, vitamin E, ginger root, grapefruit, and jasmine.

Ritual Use

Massage onto damp skin using circular motions, then rinse thoroughly. Use when you want renewal, brightness, and a refreshed body ritual experience.

Coffee Ritual Scrub

Ground • Awaken • Restore

This scrub is designed for a deeper, more grounding exfoliation. It offers a richer body ritual experience centered around texture, warmth, and embodied reset.

Ingredients

Coffee, amber powder, vitamin E, frankincense, peppermint, and copaiba.

Ritual Use

Massage onto damp skin using firm circular motions, then rinse thoroughly. Use when you want grounding, deeper exfoliation, and a fuller body reset.

Avoid introducing water directly into the container to help preserve the texture and integrity of the scrub.

FAQ

What is Ritual Restore Scrub made for?

It is made to exfoliate the skin while supporting ritual care, renewal, grounding, and restoration through touch and intentional use.

What is the difference between the two scrubs?

The Sugar + Epsom Ritual Scrub is brighter and more renewing in feel. The Coffee Ritual Scrub is deeper, more grounding, and more textured.

How often should I use the scrub?

Use as needed for body exfoliation. Adjust frequency based on your skin’s needs and sensitivity.

How do I use it?

Apply to damp skin, massage in circular motions, and rinse thoroughly. Follow with your preferred ELEKTRON body ritual product.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Ritual Restore Scrub is for external use only.
