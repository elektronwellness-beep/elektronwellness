Amber Ritual Oil

Amber Ritual Oil is a pure amber-infused body oil created to support nourishment, grounding, and intentional ritual through simple, elemental care.

Each bottle is made with amber-infused avocado oil and includes a small piece of amber inside, creating both a physical and symbolic connection to the ritual itself. The oil is meant to be more than a body product. It is a daily object of return, warmth, and embodied presence.

Ingredients

Amber-infused avocado oil, with a small piece of amber in each bottle.

Ritual Use

Warm a small amount between your hands and press into the skin as part of your daily ritual. Use after bathing, before rest, or anytime you want to return to a more grounded and intentional state.

FAQ

What is Amber Ritual Oil made for?

It is made to nourish the skin while also serving as a grounding ritual product centered on amber, touch, and intentional care.

What makes this oil different?

The oil is infused with amber and each bottle contains a small piece of amber, making it both a body oil and a symbolic ritual object.

How do I use it?

Apply a small amount to the skin and press in with intention. It can be used after bathing, during meditation, before rest, or anytime you want a grounding point in your day.

Is this a heavily scented oil?

No. This product is centered on the amber-infused avocado oil itself rather than a strong essential oil blend.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. Amber Ritual Oil is for external use only.
