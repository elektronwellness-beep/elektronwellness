Ritual Use

Warm a small amount between your hands and press into the skin as part of your daily ritual. Use after bathing, before rest, or anytime you want to return to a grounded and intentional state.

FAQ

What is Amber Ritual Oil made for?

It is made to nourish the skin while also serving as a grounding ritual product centered on amber and intentional care.

What makes this oil different?

Each bottle contains a real piece of amber, making it both a body oil and a symbolic ritual object.

Is it strongly scented?

No. This oil focuses on the natural properties of the amber-infused avocado oil rather than heavy essential oil blends.

How should I store it?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat and sunlight.

Is it for external use only?

Yes. This product is for external use only.
