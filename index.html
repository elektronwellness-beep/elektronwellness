The Ritual

  1. Arrive — three slow breaths.
  2. Hold amber — anchor to time.
  3. Choose one offering — cream, scrub, tea, oil, or stone.
  4. Listen — warmth, texture, aroma.
  5. Close — grounded, empowered, whole.
