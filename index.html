Resin Root — Details

Tumbled Baltic amber fossil (random selection)

Resin Root is your physical ritual anchor — something you can hold when your mind is loud and your body needs to return to the present.

Ritual use:

  • Hold the amber in your palm for 60 seconds.
  • Breathe in slowly. Exhale longer than you inhale.
  • Speak one word of intention: “Return.” “Ground.” “Here.”

Note: Each piece is naturally unique in size, tone, and inclusions.

Ember Intent — Details

Ritual anointing oil • 5 ml / 10 ml

Ember Intent is a “begin again” ritual — a simple anointing practice to mark your intention, soften your nervous system, and start from a centered place.

Ritual use:

  • Place a small amount on pulse points or hands.
  • Inhale slowly and visualize warmth in the body.
  • Say your intention out loud (or write it down immediately after).

If you have sensitivities, patch test first.
