About

ELEKTRON is a ritual-based wellness studio rooted in amber, time, and intentional care. Our offerings are designed to support presence through warmth, touch, scent, and pause— a return to the body and a steadying of the moment.

The Ritual

  1. Pause and breathe slowly.
  2. Hold your amber stone (or place it nearby).
  3. Apply body care with intention—or prepare a warm cup of tea.
  4. Notice warmth, texture, aroma, and stillness.
  5. Return grounded, empowered, whole.
