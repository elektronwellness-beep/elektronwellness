About

ELEKTRON is a ritual-based wellness studio rooted in amber, time, and intentional care. Our offerings steady the senses through warmth, touch, scent, and pause.

Not a rush. A return.

The Ritual

  1. Arrive. Pause. Three slow breaths.
  2. Hold amber. Keep your stone nearby as an anchor.
  3. Choose one offering. Cream, scrub, tea, oil, or fossil.
  4. Listen. Warmth. Texture. Aroma. Stillness.
  5. Close. Grounded. Empowered. Whole.
