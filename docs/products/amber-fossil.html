Amber Fossil

Indonesian blue amber. A ritual artifact of ancient resin and blue fire.

Purpose

Amber Fossil is a physical ritual object formed from Indonesian blue amber — fossilized resin known for revealing a natural blue fluorescence in sunlight.

Place it within your ritual space, keep it on the altar, or hold it during reflection, grounding, or intention work. Its presence creates a point of stillness — a material reminder that return begins in the body.

Material

ELEKTRON works with Indonesian blue amber selected for its natural visual depth and solar blue fire. Each piece carries its own shape, surface, and internal character.

Because this is a natural fossil material, each piece will vary in tone, texture, and formation.
