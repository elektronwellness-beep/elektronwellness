Ritual Invitation Box

A ceremonial container for your method.

Purpose

The Ritual Invitation Box creates boundary and continuity — a physical container that reinforces order, seriousness, and return.

It is designed to hold the tools of practice with intention, giving the ritual a dedicated place to begin and to end. The box becomes part of the method itself — a quiet structure that supports consistency over time.

Function

Use it to store your ELEKTRON ritual objects, grounding pieces, written reflections, and supporting materials between sessions. Closing the box marks completion. Opening it marks return.
