Clear Current

8oz ritual clearing mist. Prepare the field.

Purpose

Clear Current is an atmospheric ritual mist designed to prepare the space before practice.

Ritual begins with transition — the moment when the ordinary environment becomes intentional. Clear Current is used to refresh the sensory field, cleanse the immediate area, and signal that it is time to slow down and arrive.

Mist lightly through the ritual area, around the body, or anywhere presence is needed. The act itself becomes the beginning — a simple gesture that separates noise from intention.

How to Use

  1. Stand in your ritual area. Pause before beginning.
  2. Mist the space lightly. Refresh the atmosphere around you.
  3. Take three slow breaths. Let the transition settle.
  4. Begin the ritual. Continue with journaling, anointing, reflection, or return.

Clear Current may also be used anytime you wish to refresh the atmosphere or restore focus.
