The ELEKTRON Ritual

The ritual is simple by design. It is meant to be repeated until it becomes a baseline — a lifestyle commitment of intended self-care.

  1. Arrive. Pause. Three slow breaths.
  2. Anchor. Hold your amber or place it near you.
  3. Anoint. Mark the beginning with Ember Intent.
  4. Embodiment. Use cream or scrub slowly — let pace be the practice.
  5. Close. One final breath. Grounded. Whole.
