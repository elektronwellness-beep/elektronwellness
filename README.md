## The ELEKTRON Ritual

1. **Arrive.** Pause. Three slow breaths.
2. **Anchor.** Hold your amber or place it near you.
3. **Anoint.** Mark the beginning with oil.
4. **Embodiment.** Choose cream, scrub, or tea — and move slowly.
5. **Close.** One final breath. Grounded. Empowered. Whole.
